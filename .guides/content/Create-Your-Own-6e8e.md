## Create Your Own

Apply what you've noticed to your own game. Think about:

- Can a new player tell what the game is about from the start screen?
- Is the score easy to read? Is it in a useful place?
- Are your sprites a good size for the game area?
- Does the game over screen tell the player what happened and what to do next?
- Does adding a background image change how the game feels?

Make at least three visible improvements.
